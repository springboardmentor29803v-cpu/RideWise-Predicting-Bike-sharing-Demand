=======================================
Bike Sharing Dataset - Data Preprocessing
=======================================

This folder contains the dataset and preprocessing steps for the 
RideWise Bike Sharing Demand Prediction Project.

Files:
- day.csv: Daily bike-sharing data.
- hour.csv: Hourly bike-sharing data.
- Data_Preprocessing_EDA.ipynb: Jupyter notebook for preprocessing and EDA.

Dataset Source:
Hadi Fanaee-T, Laboratory of Artificial Intelligence and Decision Support (LIAAD),
University of Porto INESC Porto, Portugal.

Performed Tasks:
- Data cleaning (handling missing values, duplicates)
- Feature analysis (temperature, humidity, windspeed, etc.)
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Correlation and visualization
